ALPHA KAPPA PSI

This Co-Ed pre-professional business fraternity has been one of the most prominent pre-business organizations in the world. In helping students develop and foster their interest in business, members have not only become powerful and influential leaders in business, but also in politics, medicine, and even academia.

OVERVIEW

Founded in 1999, The Pi Tau Chapter of AKPsi has gone on to become on of the most highly regarded chapters of AKPsi across the United States. The active membership within the Pi Tau Chapter, filled with talented and multi-faceted individuals encapsures the Liberal Arts vision of our institution.

OUR MISSION

1. To further the individual welfare of its members;
2. To foster scientific research in the fields of commerce, accounts and finance;
3. To educate the public to appreciate and demand higher ideals therein; and
4. To promote and advance in institutions of college rank, courses leading to degrees in business.

BROTHERHOOD

Our brotherhood, which stands just a shade over 80 members, not only fosters a community that strives for professional success but also fosters a community that comes together to create an unparalleled social fabric in the midst of an ever-changing Stanford student body.
